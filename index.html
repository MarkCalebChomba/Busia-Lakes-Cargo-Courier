About Busia Lakes Cargo & Courier

Founded in 2010, Busia Lakes Cargo & Courier has established itself as a trusted logistics partner in East Africa. Based at the strategic Busia border between Kenya and Uganda, we leverage our location to provide seamless cross-border logistics services.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering exceptional service with integrity, reliability, and efficiency. We understand the unique challenges of regional logistics and offer customized solutions to meet our clients' diverse needs.
